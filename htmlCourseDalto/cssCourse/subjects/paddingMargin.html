Margin

The margin CSS shorthand property sets the margin area on all four sides of an element.

Look how at the moment we are setting 10px margin items are separating among them.

Another example!

It's the same functionality, we are separating 4 div boxes with margin property. It's important to know margins doesn't add up to each other instead the larger margin remains.

Let's take a look to margin collapse

The top and bottom margins of blocks are sometimes combined (collapsed) into a single margin whose size is the largest of the individual margins (or just one of them, if they are equal), a behavior known as margin collapsing.
